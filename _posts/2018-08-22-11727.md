https://www.acmicpc.net/problem/11726

```python3
N = int(input())
d = [0] * (N+1)

d[1] = 1
d[2] = 3
for i in range(3, N+1):
    tmp = d[i-1] + 2 * d[i-2]
    d[i] = tmp % 10007
print(d[N]% 10007)
```
